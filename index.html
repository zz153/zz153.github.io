About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Otago's Department of Pharmacy, working at the intersection of RNA biology, cancer genomics, and computational bioinformatics. My research focuses on developing lipid nanoparticle encapsulations for small molecules, RNA-based therapeutic strategies for brain cancers, multi-omics biomarker pipelines, and computational vaccine design.

My journey has taken me from biomedical sciences in Sheffield to molecular pathology in Leicester, clinical lab training in Muscat and Karachi, and now to the cutting edge of RNA therapeutics in Dunedin. Alongside my research, I work as a Support Worker at Community Care Trust, providing person-centred care that grounds my science in real human impact.

I bridge academia and biotechnology — translating discoveries into real-world applications.

Research & Projects

From wet lab RNA therapeutics to computational pipelines — spanning academic research, open-source tools, and translational science.

🧬

RNA Therapeutics for Brain Cancer

Contributing to MBIE-funded programmes in the Department of Pharmacy, developing lipid nanoparticle encapsulations for small molecules and RNA-based therapeutic strategies for brain cancers and Parkinson's disease.

MBIE Funded
💉

Computational Vaccine Design

Building multi-lineage influenza vaccine pipelines (H1N1, H3N2, VicB) using phylogenetics, evolutionary distance analysis, and immunoinformatics through the NZ RNA Platform Grant.

NZ RNA Platform
🎯

GBM Neoantigen Discovery

Computational pipeline for clonal neoantigen identification in IDH-wildtype GBM using TCGA mutation data, VAF-based clonality filtering, and NetMHCpan-4.2 HLA binding prediction.

Preprint 2025
🔗

ceRNA Networks in Brain Cancer

Published analysis of competing endogenous RNA networks revealing clinically relevant hub lncRNAs in GBM across TCGA and GTEx cohorts using ENCORI and miRTarBase interaction data.

Published 2026
📊

Survival Biomarker Frameworks

Reproducible cross-cohort survival biomarker discovery pipeline integrating TCGA, GTEx, and CGGA with Cox regression, permutation benchmarking, and meta-analysis for robust biomarker identification.

Open Source
